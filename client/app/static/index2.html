Wallet Operations


The wallet manages your accounts locally.
It allows you to create private and public key pairs and store the private keys safely. All private keys are encrypted using a password that you choose and cannot be accessed by anyone who doesn't have your password. The private keys never leave your computer and are only used locally.
To perform any of the DEX operations, you will first have to unlock your account using your password.

  • Account Stats


DEX Operations


The DEX component is what you are here for. It allows you to perform direct trades with tokens (buying and selling), convert between tokens and ethers from the liquidity provided to the pool (after the liquidity pool is initialized with some ETH and tokens) and to provide or burn LQT.
To use any of the DEX opearions, first make sure you have your (funded) unlocked account address in hand.
To starte using the DEX, please make sure to perform the following 3 steps in that order:
1. Initalize the DEX ('Initialize DEX' operation). This will deploy the DEX smart contract to the blockchain. .
2. Buy some tokens ('Buy Tokens' operation). You can check Account Stats to see if you already have any tokens.
3. Initialize the liquidity pool ('Initialize Liquidity Pool' operation) by providing both ETH and Tokens.
After the liquidity pool has been initialized, you can use any of the other DEX operations.

  • Initialize DEX
  • Buy Tokens (direct trade)
  • Initialize Liquidity Pool

  • Sell Token (direct trade)
  • Liquidity Pool Stats
  • Add Liquidity
  • Burn Liquidity (LQT)
  • Swap ETH for Tokens
  • Swap Tokens for ETH
