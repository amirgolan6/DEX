Initialize Liquidity Pool

Here the first liquidity provider will invest in the DEX pool, by depositing some amount of both ETH and Token, which will set the initial exchange rate.
This exchange rate, the liquidity invariant Balance(ETH) * Balance(Tokens) constant, will be maintained throughout any aditional liquidity providing or burning.

The initial LQT amount will be set with a 1:5 proportion with the ETH amount provided.
