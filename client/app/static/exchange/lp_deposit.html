Add Liquidity to Pool

In order to provide liquidity to the pool, you can first get the current ETH/Token ratio and LQT balance (must enter an unlocked account for this).
Then, enter ETH amount (can be fractional). The number of tokens required to match the ratio will be calculated and presented after investing.
Providing liquidity will grant you liquidity tokens (LQT) which you can later burn. You can view you LQT balance in Account Stats page.
